Cervidae Health: Mortality and Spatial Distribution of E. coli in White-tailed Deer

University of Florida | Emerging Pathogens Institute | 2023 – Present

Overview

Brief 2–3 sentence summary of the project question, why it matters, and the key finding.

Methods

  1. Specimen collection and necropsy from farmed white-tailed deer across Florida surveillance program.
  2. Bacterial isolation, culture, and phenotypic characterization of E. coli isolates.
  3. Whole-genome sequencing on Illumina platform; assembly and quality control.
  4. Phylogenetic reconstruction and comparative genomics to assess isolate diversity.
  5. Spatial analysis in R and ArcGIS Pro to map mortality events and pathogen distribution across farm network.
